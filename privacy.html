Effective date: July 2, 2026

Fluenta Stories Development Team (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Fluenta Stories (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in to the App, we may collect information such as:

  • your email address;
  • your name, if provided;
  • authentication-related information needed to create and manage your account.

If you sign in using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we may receive account information from these services, such as your email address and name, depending on the information you choose to share and the settings provided by Google or Apple.

Apple Sign-In may allow you to share your real email address or use a private relay email address provided by Apple.

App Usage Information

To provide the App’s features, we may collect and store information related to your activity in the App, including:

  • selected language preferences;
  • saved or favorite audio exercises;
  • learning progress or interaction history;
  • subscription or access status, if paid features are introduced in the future.

Technical Information

We may collect technical information about your device and usage of the App, such as:

  • device type and operating system;
  • app version;
  • error logs and crash reports;
  • general usage data needed to improve performance and fix issues.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as health information, political opinions, religious beliefs, or precise location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • provide access to the App’s features;
  • save your preferences and learning progress;
  • personalize your experience in the App;
  • provide translated subtitles and other language-related features;
  • improve the App, fix bugs, and maintain security;
  • respond to your questions or support requests;
  • manage subscriptions or paid features, if they are introduced in the future;
  • comply with legal obligations.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

At the moment, the App may be available without paid subscriptions. If paid features or subscriptions are introduced in the future, payments will be processed through the official app store platforms, such as Google Play or Apple App Store.

We do not directly collect or store your full payment card details. Payment processing, billing, refunds, and subscription management are handled by the relevant app store according to its own terms and privacy policy.

We may use trusted third-party services to operate and improve the App. These services may process limited information on our behalf.

These services may include:

  • DigitalOcean, for hosting our backend infrastructure and storing or delivering app content such as audio files, images, subtitles, and related data;
  • Google Analytics for Firebase, for app usage analytics;
  • OpenAI services, for generating translations of app content such as subtitles and learning materials;
  • Google Sign-In, for account authentication. If you use Google Sign-In, Google may provide us with account information such as your email address and name, as described in the “Account Information” section above;
  • Apple Sign-In, if introduced in the future, for account authentication. If you use Apple Sign-In, Apple may provide us with account information such as your email address and name, or a private relay email address, depending on your Apple settings and choices;
  • Google Play services, for Android app distribution and payments, if paid features are introduced;
  • Apple App Store services, for iOS app distribution and payments, if paid features are introduced.

Third-party services may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We use these services only as needed to provide, maintain, and improve the App.

6. Data Retention

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the App’s services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain security, and enforce our agreements.

Account information and app usage data are generally kept for as long as your account remains active.

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize personal information associated with your account within a reasonable period, unless we are required to keep certain information for legal, security, fraud prevention, payment, or accounting reasons.

Some information may remain in backups, security logs, or system records for a limited period after account deletion. We generally aim to remove or anonymize such data within 90 days, unless a longer retention period is required by law or necessary for legitimate security or operational reasons.
